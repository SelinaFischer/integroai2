### ❌ Avoid
- "Strategy Call" → Use "Discovery Call"
- "Consultation" → Use "Discovery Call"
- "Schedule a meeting" → Use "Book"

## The Framework

**Always use this exact format with › separators:**

```
Discover › Design › Validate › Deliver › Iterate
```

### Framework Descriptions
| Phase | One-liner |
|-------|-----------|
| Discover | Understand your business goals and AI opportunities |
| Design | Create a tailored AI strategy and roadmap |
| Validate | Test assumptions with pilots before full commitment |
| Deliver | Implement solutions with measurable outcomes |
| Iterate | Continuously improve based on real results |

## Brand Voice & Tone

### We Are
- **Confident** — We know AI, but we're not arrogant
- **Clear** — No jargon, no buzzwords, no fluff
- **Practical** — Results over theory
- **Approachable** — Human first, consultant second
- **Honest** — We'll tell you if AI isn't right for you

### We're Not
- Overly technical with non-technical audiences
- Salesy or pushy
- Vague or full of empty promises
- Using AI hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing")

## Target Audience Language

**Who we serve:** SME leaders (CEOs, founders, operations heads)

**Their concerns:**
- "Will this actually work for my business?"
- "I don't want to waste budget on AI hype"
- "I need clarity, not more confusion"
- "Is my business even ready for AI?"

**How we address them:**
- Emphasize ROI and measurable outcomes
- Acknowledge their skepticism — it's valid
- Offer clarity and honest assessment
- Remove risk with free discovery call
